I am a PhD student at the [Global Institute of Future Technology (GIFT)](https://gift.sjtu.edu.cn/), Shanghai Jiao Tong University, supervised by [Prof. Shuangjia Zheng](https://zhenglab.sjtu.edu.cn/). I obtained my bachelor's and master's degrees from [Sun Yat-sen University](https://www.sysu.edu.cn/), where I was supervised by [Prof. Yuedong Yang](https://cse.sysu.edu.cn/en/teacher/YangYuedong).

My research interests center on **AI Agents**, **foundation models for AI4Science**, and **biological LLM applications**. I have served as an AI researcher at the [National Supercomputer Center in Guangzhou](http://www.nscc-gz.cn/), [Tencent](https://www.tencent.com/en-us/), and [Lingang Laboratory](https://www.lglab.ac.cn/).
